Tech Inferno:
In hell Wifi works really well

In hell wifi works really well is a visual-interactive narrative that takes us through nine circles of technological hell, modern interpretation of those found in the Divine Comedy by Dante Alighieri. It is a post-internet instalation, using modern web tools and 3D graphics to tell a visual story through the frame of a web browser.
Instalation was on display offline during the exibition at University in Ljubljana in May 2016.
